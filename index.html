Subscriptions

Kano Pro subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. You can cancel or manage your subscription at any time in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

AI Features

Kano includes an optional AI feature that provides training feedback based on user-provided workout history. This information is for training reflection only and does not constitute medical advice.

Data Handling

Local storage

Workout data is stored locally on your device.

AI processing

When AI is used, relevant data is processed securely via a third-party AI service.
