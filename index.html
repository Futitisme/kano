Subscriptions

Kano Pro subscriptions are managed entirely through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in Apple Subscriptions.

Privacy Policy

Kano is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not require accounts, does not use analytics, and does not collect personal data.

Local Data Storage

All workout data is stored locally on the user’s device. The developer does not have access to this data. No servers or cloud storage are used.

AI Features

Kano includes an optional AI chat feature. When the user sends a message to the AI chat, relevant workout data is sent directly to the OpenAI API solely for generating a response.

This data is not stored, tracked, or accessed by the developer, and is only transmitted at the user’s explicit request.

Data Sharing

Kano does not sell, share, or monetize user data. No data is used for advertising or tracking purposes.
