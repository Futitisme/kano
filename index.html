Support

Kano is a minimalist iOS workout logger for strength training and progress tracking, with optional AI-based training reflections.

Contact

Email: support@kano.app

Subscriptions

Kano Pro subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Apple Subscriptions.

AI Features

AI feedback is optional and based solely on user-provided workout data. It does not constitute medical advice.

Privacy Policy

Kano respects your privacy. This policy explains how data is handled within the app.

Data Collection

  • Workout data entered by the user
  • Exercise names, sets, repetitions, and weights
  • Optional AI chat messages

Data Usage

Data is used solely to provide app functionality, workout tracking, and optional AI-based training feedback.

AI Processing

When AI features are used, relevant data is securely processed via a third-party AI service (OpenAI API). Data is not used for advertising or tracking.

Data Storage

Workout data is stored locally on your device. Kano does not sell or share personal data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@kano.app
